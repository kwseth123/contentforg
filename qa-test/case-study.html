Chapter 01

Client Profile

Meridian Manufacturing is a $520M precision parts manufacturer with 3,800 employees across 6 facilities. They serve automotive OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers, processing over 2 million orders annually.

Chapter 02

The Challenge

Meridian was struggling with:

  • 12 disconnected systems requiring manual data entry at each handoff

  • Month-end close taking 5+ days with 3 full-time staff dedicated to reconciliation

  • 18% defect rate in cross-system data transfers causing production delays

  • $1.8M annual compliance remediation costs from inconsistent reporting

  • No visibility into real-time production metrics for executive team
18% staff dedicated reconciliation
80% Month End Close
$520M value
Chapter 03

The Solution

Acme Solutions deployed a comprehensive automation platform in 78 days: Phase 1: Connected SAP ERP and Siemens MES for real-time production data streaming
Phase 2: Deployed 23 automated workflows replacing manual data entry
Phase 3: Launched executive dashboards with live KPIs across all 6 facilities
Phase 4: Implemented automated compliance reporting for ISO 9001 and IATF 16949
