1.

Company Background

GlobalTech Industries is a mid-market manufacturer ($340M revenue, 2,500 employees) headquartered in Detroit, MI. They produce precision components for automotive and aerospace sectors. Recently acquired a smaller competitor (Atlas Precision) and are now integrating two separate ERP systems.

2.

Key Attendees

Sarah Chen — VP Operations (Decision Maker)

  • 8 years at GlobalTech, promoted to VP 2 years ago

  • LinkedIn: Posted about "digital transformation fatigue" last month

  • Known pain: Manual reporting consuming her team's bandwidth James Park — Director of IT (Technical Evaluator)

  • Background in SAP and Oracle ERP implementations

  • Likely concerned about integration complexity and timeline

  • Will want to see API documentation and security certifications
3.

Discovery Questions

Pain Discovery:

  • "Walk me through what happens when a production order comes in — how many systems does it touch?"

  • "How much time does your team spend on month-end reconciliation?"

  • "What keeps you up at night about the Atlas integration?" Impact Quantification:

  • "If you could reclaim those 14 hours/week per manager, what would that team focus on?"

  • "What does a compliance gap cost you in audit remediation?" Decision Process:

  • "Beyond you and James, who else needs to weigh in?"

  • "What does your evaluation timeline look like?"
4.

Competitive Intelligence

  • GlobalTech evaluated AutoFlow 18 months ago but did not move forward (unknown reason)

  • Their SAP environment suggests they may be considering SAP's native automation tools

  • Atlas Precision was using ManuTech — likely under contract through Q3

  • Key differentiator to emphasize: Our dual-ERP integration capability is unique
5.

Meeting Agenda

  1. Introductions & Context (5 min) — Confirm attendees and objectives

  2. Current State Discovery (15 min) — Systems landscape, pain points, manual processes

  3. Impact Quantification (10 min) — Tie pains to dollars and time

  4. Solution Preview (10 min) — Brief demo of real-time integration

  5. Evaluation Process (5 min) — Timeline, stakeholders, next steps

  6. Action Items (5 min) — Confirm follow-up commitments
