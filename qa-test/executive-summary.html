Executive Overview

GlobalTech Industries is experiencing significant operational inefficiency driven by disconnected systems and manual processes. Our analysis identifies $2.4M in annual waste that can be eliminated through workflow automation, with a projected 4.2x ROI in the first year.

02 / 05

Key Findings

  • $2.4M annual waste from manual data reconciliation across 12 systems

  • 14 hours/week per manager spent on reporting tasks that should be automated

  • 23% error rate in cross-system data transfers leading to compliance exposure

  • 6.2 day average for month-end close vs. industry benchmark of 2 days

  • Zero real-time visibility into production metrics for leadership

Recommended Solution

We recommend a phased deployment of the Acme Solutions platform: Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Connect ERP and MES for real-time production data
Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Deploy automated reporting and compliance engine
Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): Roll out no-code workflow builder to operations team Total investment: $285,000 | Expected annual savings: $1,200,000
